Let Us Enhance Your Database Experience!

At BBS, your security and peace of mind are our top priorities. We kindly request that you submit your database through our dedicated customer service channels, either before or after making your payment. Please share your database via a Google Drive or OneDrive link. Rest assured, our expert team will only begin work once your payment is confirmed, safeguarding your information every step of the way. Before you proceed, we encourage you to back up your existing database. Modifying it without careful consideration can lead to unforeseen issues that may impact your operations. We accept MySQL and SQLite databases in their original export format, neatly compressed in a ZIP file. Your journey towards an optimized database begins with us! For more details about our processes, please refer to our Terms and Conditions. Together, we can ensure that your database evolves in a secure and efficient manner, unlocking its true potential.

Payment Information

We are committed to providing transparent, value-driven solutions tailored to your needs. Our modification fees are structured based on the complexity and size of your database, ensuring that you only pay for the services that best fit your requirements:
  • $119 for databases with fewer than 4,000 records.
  • $149 for databases with 5 to 8 tables (or up to 5,000 records).
  • $849 for databases with 8 to 12 tables (or up to 25,000 records).
  • $1,999 for databases with more than 12 tables (or exceeding 60,000 records).
Your investment with BBS is backed by our 7-day satisfaction guarantee, giving you the flexibility to request adjustments to ensure that your modifications perfectly align with your business goals. Please note that a 10% administrative fee will apply to any refunds.
We currently support modifications for MySQL and SQLite databases only. All files should be submitted in their original export format, compressed into a ZIP file, ensuring a smooth process without the need for direct access to your database server. We recommend sending the database in an unencrypted ZIP format to facilitate modifications. If encryption is applied, you may optionally provide a document with the necessary passwords for the encrypted columns. While this is not mandatory—since encryptions usually only apply to certain columns—sharing these passwords will help us work more efficiently with your data, should the need arise.
