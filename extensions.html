Enhance Your Database with Extensions!

At BBS, your security and peace of mind are our top priorities. We kindly request that you submit your database through our dedicated customer service channels, either before or after making your payment. Please share your database via a Google Drive or OneDrive link. Rest assured, our expert team will only begin work once your payment is confirmed, safeguarding your information every step of the way. Before you proceed, we encourage you to back up your existing database. Modifying it without careful consideration can lead to unforeseen issues that may impact your operations. We accept MySQL and SQLite databases in their original export format, neatly compressed in a ZIP file. Your journey towards an optimized database begins with us! For more details about our processes, please refer to our Terms and Conditions. Together, we can ensure that your database evolves in a secure and efficient manner, unlocking its true potential.

Important Policies:

We understand the importance of flexibility for your business. That's why every plan comes with a 3-day satisfaction guarantee, offering you up to two complimentary modifications within this period. Please keep in mind that if you independently alter the database after the extension, this guarantee may be voided, as we strive to ensure the integrity and security of your data at all times.
  • Basic Plan: Includes up to 4 additional tables or 8 extra columns to perfectly match your evolving needs.
  • Standard Plan: Includes up to 4 additional tables or 20 extra columns for more extensive database customizations.
  • Business Plan: Offers unlimited modifications, ensuring that your database grows seamlessly with your business.
To enhance your database further, you may also request the addition of temporary tables and common table expressions (CTEs) as part of your modifications, allowing for even greater flexibility and efficiency in managing your data.
