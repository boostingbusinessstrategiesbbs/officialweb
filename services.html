Do you have a satisfaction guarantee?+

Yes, we are committed to your satisfaction. We offer a satisfaction guarantee on all our database projects, including up to 3 free revisions within a 7-day period, to ensure you are completely satisfied with the final product. Your satisfaction is our unwavering commitment.

What happens if I need further modifications after the guarantee period?+

If you require additional modifications after the guarantee period, we are happy to assist for a reasonable fee. Our goal is to provide ongoing support and ensure that your database continues to serve your business needs effectively, dedicated to your long-term success.

What is the guarantee period for extensions?+

The guarantee period for extensions varies by plan. For Basic and Standard plans, you receive 3 days of guarantee with up to 2 modifications per order. In the Business plan, you have unlimited changes during the 3-day guarantee period. Modifications of databases from other providers or developers come with a 7-day guarantee with up to 10 modifications if they are complex and have more than 5,000 records. For databases with 0 to 4,000 records, the guarantee period is 7 days with up to 5 modifications.

How will my database be delivered?+

Your database will be delivered via email, providing a shareable link through Google Drive or One Drive, according to your preference. You will have 7 days to access the order and confirm receipt. If we do not hear from you within that time, we will send reminders to help you remember to download it.

Do you use encryption for sensitive data?+

Yes, we utilize AES_ENCRYPT to encrypt columns containing sensitive data in MySQL databases when necessary. We will also provide you with the passwords and a PDF guide with practical instructions on database encryption.

What do I need to do to send my database for modifications and expansions?+

Please send your database using a shareable link from Google Drive or One Drive for security purposes. This ensures that your data is handled securely during the modification and expansion process.
